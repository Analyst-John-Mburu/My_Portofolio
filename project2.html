Project 2

Advanced Bank Loan Data Analysis

Traditional reporting methods in loan data analysis lack interactivity and depth, making it difficult for decision-makers to gain comprehensive insights into lending operations, borrower behavior, and loan performance. This limitation hampers strategic planning and the ability to respond effectively to financial trends.

Objective

  • Design and develop interconnected, dynamic dashboards.
  • Provide holistic, real-time insights into loan data.
  • Empower decision-makers with actionable intelligence.
  • Visualize key performance indicators (KPIs).
  • Analyze borrower demographics and loan metrics.
  • Highlight financial trends effectively.

    • KEY INSIGHTS

    • Loan Application Trends: Steady Month-over-Month (MoM) increase in loan applications indicates growing demand for financial products.
    • Disbursement Patterns: Cyclical funding behaviors highlight periods of high capital outflow, aiding forecasting and resource allocation.
    • Repayment and Cash Flow Monitoring: Trends in total and Month-to-Date (MTD) amounts received assess borrower repayment behavior and cash inflow consistency.
    • Interest Rate Analysis: Monitoring average interest rates provides insights into lending profitability and market competitiveness.
    • Borrower Financial Resilience: Average Debt-to-Income (DTI) ratio offers a snapshot of borrower capacity and lending risk.
